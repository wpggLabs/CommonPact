# Contributor Policy

Contributors affirm they have the right to submit work under the repository license. Maintainers may request provenance clarification for substantial contributions.

Contributions must not include confidential employer material, personal data without authority, excessive copied standards text, secrets, credentials, or misleading claims of certification or legal approval.

## Developer Certificate of Origin

All contributions must use `git commit -s`. By adding the `Signed-off-by` line, the contributor certifies that they wrote the contribution or have the right to submit it under Apache-2.0, consistent with the Developer Certificate of Origin 1.1.
