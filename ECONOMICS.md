# Economics

## No mandatory protocol commission

CommonPact requires no percentage of rides, rentals, deliveries, donations, or other pacts.

## Optional value can be paid

A product may charge for application features, hosting, relays, mapping, payment processing, escrow, identity checks, insurance, support, moderation, dispute services, integrations, audits, certification, or local operations.

## Disclosure expectations

Before agreement, clients should disclose participant and provider fees, commissions, sponsored ranking, required third parties, data monetization, and cancellation or refund rules.

## Avoiding hidden recentralization

An open protocol can still recentralize if one provider controls identity acceptance, discovery reach, push delivery, payment, reputation, app distribution, or certification. The project must evaluate practical dependency, not only formal optionality.
