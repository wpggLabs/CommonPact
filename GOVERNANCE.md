# Governance

`wpggLabs` is the founding maintainer during the initial research phase.

Material changes should identify a concrete problem, provide prior art or evidence, state privacy/security/compatibility/profile effects, include migration where needed, receive public review, and update schemas, vectors, status, and website claims together.

CommonPact governs the shared core and profile-admission rules. Profiles may have independent maintainers. Registry inclusion does not give core maintainers authority over a profile's operations, legal decisions, or community.

New maintainers should demonstrate sustained contribution, claim discipline, respect for profile boundaries, security and privacy judgment, willingness to document disagreement, and no undisclosed control conflict.

No sponsor, provider, application, token holder, infrastructure operator, or founder receives permanent protocol control because of funding or usage. Apache-2.0 permits forks; trademark rules distinguish independent work from official endorsement.
