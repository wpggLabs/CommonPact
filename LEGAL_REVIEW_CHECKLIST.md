# Legal and Operational Review Checklist

This is a review framework, not legal advice.

Before any real deployment, accountable parties SHOULD identify:

## Protocol and software

- license and notice obligations;
- contributor provenance and patents;
- trademark and certification claims;
- data-controller, processor, and retention roles;
- export, deletion, subpoena, and breach procedures;
- consumer disclosure and electronic-signature requirements.

## Domain operation

- required licenses, permits, registrations, and insurance;
- worker, contractor, employment, and cooperative classification;
- accessibility and nondiscrimination obligations;
- age, capacity, guardianship, and consent requirements;
- prohibited goods, services, or participants;
- incident, emergency, and law-enforcement procedures;
- jurisdiction, venue, and dispute terms.

## Payments and funds

- money transmission, escrow, custody, sanctions, AML, fraud, tax, chargeback, and refund obligations;
- charitable solicitation and nonprofit rules for donation profiles;
- who holds funds and who bears loss;
- retention of receipts and regulatory records.

## Pilot gate

No pilot should proceed without named responsibility for operations, support, safety, privacy, legal review, payment handling, insurance, incident response, and shutdown authority.
