# Monetization

CommonPact does not automatically earn money when another party uses the idea or protocol.

Potential revenue may come from official applications, hosted relays, enterprise integration, support, consulting, audits, conformance testing, authorized certification, training, documentation, optional lawful services, partnerships, grants, sponsorships, or separately licensed software.

Revenue should come from optional value, not a hidden mandatory protocol toll.

Any certification or trademark program must publish criteria, conflicts, fees, appeal paths, and the difference between technical compatibility and safety or legal approval.
