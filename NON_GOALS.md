# Non-Goals

CommonPact does not attempt to:

- build one application for every transaction;
- eliminate all businesses or intermediaries;
- guarantee zero fees;
- provide global identity verification;
- decide legal identity, licensing, insurance, tax, charity, labor, or consumer-protection status;
- hold or settle funds;
- guarantee delivery, asset condition, transportation safety, or truthful campaign use;
- create a universal reputation score;
- provide one global moderation policy;
- operate emergency response;
- replace courts, regulators, insurers, banks, payment networks, or local operators;
- require blockchain consensus, cryptocurrency, or a token;
- make Bluetooth mesh a universal internet replacement;
- guarantee adoption, density, or counterparties;
- make every profile wire-compatible with PactRide v0.1;
- freeze the protocol permanently.

CommonPact may define references and evidence for external systems without claiming to become those systems.
