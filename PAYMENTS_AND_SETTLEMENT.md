# Payments and Settlement

CommonPact does not hold funds or require one payment method.

A profile may include amount and currency, deposits, escrow requirements, schedules, refund conditions, donation restrictions, external payment references, settlement declarations, and dispute references.

## Settlement declarations

A signed declaration records what an actor claims about settlement. It is not bank or payment-network finality unless accompanied by verifiable external evidence.

## Optional providers

Applications may integrate cash, bank transfer, cards, wallets, escrow, regulated custodians, invoicing, or institutional disbursement. Fees and dependencies should be disclosed before agreement.

## Financial boundary

Profiles involving custody, donations, lending, investment, money transmission, or pooled funds require specialist legal and operational review. A CommonPact event does not authorize regulated activity.
