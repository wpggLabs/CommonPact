# Profile Registry

## Purpose

The repository may maintain a human-readable registry of known profile identifiers and status.

Registry inclusion means only that an identifier and documentation location are known. It does not imply endorsement, safety, legality, quality, certification, or operational availability.

## Initial registry

| Identifier | Project | Status | Repository |
|---|---|---|---|
| `pactride` | PactRide | Standalone founder-vision-complete research RFC | https://github.com/wpggLabs/PactRide |
| `pact-rental` | PactRental | Complete founder-authored Research RFC package; independent evidence incomplete | `profiles/pactrental/` |
| `pact-delivery` | PactDelivery | Concept | `profiles/pactdelivery/` |
| `pact-fund` | PactFund | High-risk concept pending professional review | `profiles/pactfund/` |

## Identifier rules

- lowercase ASCII;
- stable and descriptive;
- no misleading claim of official authority;
- versioned independently;
- no reuse of retired identifiers for incompatible meaning.

A future decentralized registry or discovery mechanism is outside the v0.1 core.
