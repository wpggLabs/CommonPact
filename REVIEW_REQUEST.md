# Independent Review Request

CommonPact is a pre-implementation research RFC. This repository seeks falsifiable, evidence-backed review; it does not seek endorsements or declarations of production readiness.

## Useful review scopes

- protocol semantics, canonicalization, lifecycle, schemas, and test vectors;
- privacy and metadata leakage;
- cryptography and key-management assumptions;
- interoperability and transport behavior;
- accessibility, abuse, governance, or domain-specific boundaries;
- licensing, contributor provenance, and trademark-policy clarity.

## Review submission

Open a GitHub issue or pull request that identifies the exact repository commit, review scope, evidence or reproduction steps, severity, conflicts of interest, and recommended change. Maintainers record accepted reviews and dispositions in `REVIEW_LOG.md`.

Reviews must not include personal data, credentials, confidential material, or unsupported claims of certification, safety, or legal approval.
