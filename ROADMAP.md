# Roadmap

The roadmap is evidence-gated, not date-driven.

## Phase 0 — Complete founder vision

**Status: complete.** Vision, journeys, protocol, profile system, second-domain documentation, risks, economics, governance, schemas, fixtures, website, and completion boundaries are public.

## Phase 1 — Founder-authored protocol core

**Status: complete as a draft research baseline.** Normative validation order, error codes, strict event payload definitions, cryptographic fixtures, PactRide mapping, and PactRental Research RFC package exist.

Exit to the next maturity label still requires substantive external review and resolution of critical ambiguities.

## Phase 2 — Independent conformance

- two languages;
- independently written validators;
- reproduced event IDs and signatures;
- valid and invalid vector agreement;
- cross-client export/import;
- duplicate, reordering, expiry, downgrade, signer, and conflict tests.

## Phase 3 — Transport evidence

- one precise internet carrier profile;
- relay/client compatibility matrix;
- encrypted negotiation fixtures;
- retry, outage, duplicate, and sender-copy measurements;
- metadata analysis;
- nearby exchange experiments with bounded claims.

## Phase 4 — Profile validation

- independent PactRide mapping review;
- fictional PactRental implementation;
- PactDelivery custody research;
- professional financial and legal review before PactFund advances.

## Phase 5 — Bounded pilot consideration

Only with an accountable operator, legal and insurance review, accessibility plan, safety and incident process, support ownership, funding, named maintainers, narrow scope, measurements, and stop conditions.

## Phase 6 — Standards or maintenance decision

The project may become an active standards effort, a maintained research reference, a profile family, or an archived public design record. No phase promises launch.
