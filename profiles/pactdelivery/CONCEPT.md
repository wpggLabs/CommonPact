# PactDelivery Concept

**Status:** candidate concept, not operational

PactDelivery explores sender, courier, subcontractor, recipient, and return-to-sender coordination. It must define custody handoffs, signer sets, address and route privacy, proof of delivery, refusal, loss, damage, prohibited goods, carrier liability, and jurisdiction before becoming a draft profile.

See issue #6 for the required research. CommonPact does not currently claim a safe or lawful delivery protocol.
