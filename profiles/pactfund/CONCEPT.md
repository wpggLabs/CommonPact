# PactFund High-Risk Concept

**Status:** concept pending professional financial and legal review

PactFund explores donation intent, campaign terms, release conditions, expenditure evidence, refunds, and beneficiary privacy. CommonPact must not hold, route, or guarantee funds and must not imply charitable status.

Any advancement requires review of custody, money transmission, sanctions, AML, fraud, tax, solicitation, chargebacks, restricted campaigns, data retention, and cross-border law. See issue #7.
